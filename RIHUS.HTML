Microsoft Word ke Common Terms

  1. Font: Style aur appearance jaise Arial, Times New Roman.
  2. Alignment: Text position - left, center, right.
  3. Margin: Page edge aur content ke beech ka space.
  4. Paragraph: Group of sentences.
  5. Header/Footer: Page ke upar/neeche extra info.
  6. Bullet/Numbering: Lists ke liye use hota hai.
  7. Table: Rows/columns mein data organize karna.
  8. Image: Photos ya graphics insert karna.

Important Tools

  1. Spell Check: Spelling mistakes detect karta hai.
  2. Grammar Check: Grammar errors highlight karta hai.
  3. Track Changes: Document editing track karta hai.
  4. Comments: Notes ya feedback dene ke liye.
